About

Our Timely Goal productivity app is an easy to use tool that helps you to build a routine of positive life changing habits, and planning your schedule to keep you on track. Our app allows you to set personal goals, track your progress, and keep you motivated.

Goal

The primary goal is to help users to plan, focus, and create the results that they want in any area in their life. Our user friendly interface makes it simple and intuitive to use while users can stay effective, hit their target and get the best results.

Features & Benefits

Customization

The app is fully customizable. You can fully customize goals, colors, and sounds.

Habit Management

Create, complete, stop or pause your habits whenever it is convenient for you.

Statistics

Our statistic feature can track habits and analyze progress and keep the streak going.

Reminders

Set up recurring reminders for things you need to do (daily, weekly, monthly).

Suggestions

Based on your statistics and previous time entries, the app will give you suggestions on how to perform better on tasks.

Rewards

Users can unlock the new features or earm points which can be redeemed for free food or drinks when they reach their target goals.
